Instructions

Instructions:
• Click icon to place terrain
• Drag terrain to move
• Click terrain to select
• Press Delete to remove

Measure Tool:
• Click to activate
• Drag at least 1" to create
• Drag text to reposition
• Drag endpoints to resize
• Click measure to select
• Delete to remove

Text Tool:
• Click to activate
• Click table to place
• Type to add text
• Drag to move
• Drag corners to resize

Export Image:
• Click to export table
• Saves as 2160×1440 PNG
• All elements included

Deployment Zones:
• Click zone color to place
• Drag zone to move
• Drag corners to resize

Special Zone:
• Yellow circle (max 18")
• Drag corners to resize
