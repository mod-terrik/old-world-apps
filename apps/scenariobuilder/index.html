Instructions

  • Instructions: Click icon to place terrain • Drag terrain to move • Click terrain to select • Press Delete to remove
  • Measure Tool: Click to activate • Drag at least 1" to create • Drag text to reposition • Drag endpoints to resize • Click measure to select • Delete to remove
  • Text Tool: Click to activate • Click table to place • Type to add text • Drag to move • Drag corners to resize
  • Export Image: Click to export table • Saves as 2160×1440 PNG • All elements included
  • Deployment Zones: Click zone color • Choose horizontal or diagonal • Click table to place • Drag to move • Diagonal zones extend from corner and stop 12" from center
